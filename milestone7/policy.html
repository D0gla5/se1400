Privacy Policy

Effective Date:

At NxgenGourmet, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or make a purchase.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with:

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Update or correct your information

  • Opt out of marketing communications

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and analytics tools to collect browsing data and personalize your experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We recommend reviewing their policies before providing any personal information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
